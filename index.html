Welcome to Flash World!

Welcome to Flash World! A great website dedicated to playing iconic and fun flash games. If you're on an old device with flash support you can natively run them in flash player. If you don't have flash player you can play through ruffle emulation! You may also find some of your favorite Javascript titles.

Note for users on old devices:

1024x768 and at least 1GB of RAM recommended
